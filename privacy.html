Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 9, 2024

Overview

Athloop ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

Device Identification

  • We collect a unique device identifier (Device ID) to identify your account and sync your data across app sessions
  • This identifier is automatically generated by your iOS device and cannot be used to personally identify you

Health Data (Optional)

If you grant permission, we access the following health data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Workouts: Type, duration, distance, calories, and dates
  • Heart Rate Data: Heart rate, resting heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Cardiovascular Fitness: VO2 Max
  • Activity Metrics: Steps, flights climbed, exercise time, stand time
  • Sleep Data: Sleep analysis and duration
  • Mobility Metrics: Walking speed, step length, gait metrics
  • Body Measurements: Weight, height, body composition
  • Vital Signs: Respiratory rate, oxygen saturation, body temperature, blood pressure
Important: HealthKit access is completely optional. The app works without HealthKit permissions, though training plans will be less personalized.

Training Data

  • Fitness goals you create
  • Target dates and durations for your goals
  • Training plans generated by our AI
  • Progress tracking and session completion status

Language Preference

  • Your device's system language (French or English) is automatically detected and saved to provide localized content

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your personalized fitness goals
  • Generate AI-powered training plans adapted to your fitness level and health data
  • Track your progress toward your goals
  • Provide content in your preferred language
  • Improve the app's functionality and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Backend Storage

  • Your data is securely stored on our backend servers hosted on Railway.app
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • Data is transmitted using encrypted HTTPS connections

HealthKit Data

  • HealthKit data is never stored on our servers
  • Health data is only read from your device when generating training plans
  • Only aggregated summaries are sent to our AI service (Claude AI by Anthropic)
  • Raw health data remains on your device and is protected by iOS security

Third-Party Services

Claude AI (Anthropic)

  • We use Claude AI to analyze your fitness goals and generate personalized training plans
  • Only anonymized activity summaries are sent to Claude AI
  • No personally identifiable information is shared with Anthropic
  • Anthropic's privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

Railway.app (Hosting)

  • Our backend API is hosted on Railway.app infrastructure
  • Railway provides secure, encrypted data storage
  • Railway's privacy policy: https://railway.app/legal/privacy

Data Retention

  • Your account data remains on our servers as long as you use the app
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us
  • If you delete the app, your backend data will remain but will not be accessible without your Device ID

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw HealthKit permissions at any time through iOS Settings
  • Export your data (available upon request)

Children's Privacy

Athloop is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Email: support@athloop.app
  • Website: https://athloop.app

Apple HealthKit Specific Disclosures

In accordance with Apple's HealthKit requirements:

  • Health data is used solely for providing and improving health and fitness features
  • Health data is not used for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Health data is not sold to third parties, data brokers, or advertisers
  • Health data is not shared with third parties except as necessary to provide app functionality (anonymized summaries to Claude AI)
  • You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time through iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health
